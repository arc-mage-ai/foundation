Transparency Statement

Last updated March 2026

Who we are

FeedHope Foundation is operated by Arc Aleph LLC, a single-member LLC. This is a small, personal project—one person doing what they can to help feed kids. The LLC structure was chosen purely for administrative simplicity; there are no investors, no board, and no staff. Everything is run by one person in their spare time.

How your money is used

Of every dollar donated:

80¢

Goes directly to Mary's Meals

A charity that provides school meals in some of the world's poorest communities

20¢

Advertising & operating costs

Covers payment processing, hosting, and ads to reach more donors

Mary's Meals estimates the cost of feeding a child for a full school year at approximately 25.20, or roughly 13¢ per meal. That means an 80¢ contribution funds about 6 meals.

Why Mary's Meals?

Mary's Meals is a globally recognised charity with a low overhead model and independent financial audits. Their work is focused, verifiable, and cost-effective. We pass donations along as a batch transfer; we do not handle the meal logistics ourselves.

Don't believe us?

That's fine! Donate directly to Mary's Meals if you want to be sure your money is going to feed kids. We won't be offended. But you have to do it right now, otherwise that was just an excuse.

Honest limitations

  • Donation processing fees (typically ~2–3%) are deducted before the 80/20 split.
  • This project is not independently audited. You are trusting one person's accounting.
  • We do not currently publish a formal financial report, BUT WE WILL BY XXXXXXXXX.
