Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated March 2026

What we collect

When you donate through PayPal, we collect only what's necessary:

  • Contribution amount — to process your payment
  • Email address — PayPal provides this; we use it only for receipts

We do NOT collect or store payment card information, home addresses, or other personal details.

How we use your data

  • To process your contribution through PayPal
  • To send you a receipt (if you provided an email)
  • To comply with legal requirements (charity reporting, tax law)

We never sell or share your data.

Payment processing

PayPal handles all payment processing. Your payment information is processed by PayPal directly—we never touch your card details. PayPal's privacy practices apply to payment processing. See PayPal's Privacy Policy.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU or covered by GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — ask what data we have about you
  • Delete — request we delete your email from our records
  • Correct — ask us to fix incorrect information

Email us at support@arcaleph.llc to exercise these rights. We'll respond within 30 days.

Security & storage

  • Cookies & tracking: We don't use any. PayPal may use theirs for checkout.
  • Data retention: We keep contribution records for 7 years (legally required). After that, we delete your email address.
  • Security: Your data is transmitted securely via HTTPS. We store data on secure servers with encryption. PayPal handles payment security.
  • Updates: We may update this policy. We'll post changes here. Continued use means you accept the changes.

Terms of service

When you contribute:

  • You must be 18+ or have parental consent
  • Your contribution is final and non-refundable (except as required by law)
  • Don't attempt to hack, spam, or disrupt the site

What we do:

  • Use your contribution to feed children in need
  • Stay transparent about fund usage (see Transparency Statement)
  • Not liable for PayPal service outages or payment failures

Disputes: Contact support@arcaleph.llc first. Disputes are governed by the laws of the United States.

Questions?

Reach out at support@arcaleph.llc. We're happy to help.
